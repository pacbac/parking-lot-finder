About

Have you ever driven to your destination, only to learn that you didn't know where to park? Fear no more! Introducing Parking Lot Finder! This simple tool allows the user to find the nearest parking lot to the entered destination. To use the application, simply enter your destination. Then, select a possible parking location from the search results. The map will show you the route from your current location to the parking lot, as well as a walking route from the parking lot to your destination.
